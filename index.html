PITCH

In this interactive journey, players walk alongside Lennox, a man who’s risen from homelessness to help others. Through his eyes, you’ll experience the struggles of those living in poverty, face tough decisions, and learn the impact of small actions. By volunteering, providing aid, and making compassionate choices, you’ll uncover the harsh realities of hunger and survival. Your mission is to create understanding and raise awareness regarding homelessness and discover that no matter how bad things get, it is possible to get through with the assistance of community and compassion. Can you change Lennox's world for good?

THEME

As people play through Lennox's life, they'll be able to see firsthand the struggles of homelessness, hunger, and loneliness. Through interactive choices, they'll see the personal side of these struggles, understand that homelessness is not the result of bad decisions but of circumstances that happen to anyone. Players will participate in activities like volunteering and allocating resources, learning how little kind acts can make a lasting difference. By doing this, the game puts emphasis on compassion, empathy, and the power of support of the community to assist others through the issues of hunger.

MY WHY

I’ve always believed that empathy can change the world. After learning about homelessness, I wanted to create a game that would raise awareness and make players think differently. Lennox’s story shows how quickly life can change and that no one is immune to hardship. This game is important to me because it challenges players to walk in someone else’s shoes and experience the pain of hunger and poverty. It reminds us that no matter where we come from, we all have the potential to make a difference for those in need.
